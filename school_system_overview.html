Level 1: System Context

👥 Part 1 — Who Uses the System?

Meet the real people who interact with the application daily to keep the school running smoothly.

👨‍💼

School Admin

The supervisor of the entire system. Holds absolute control over students, staff, financial reports, and system settings.

👩‍🏫

Teacher

Manages their specific classes, records daily attendance, inputs academic results, and communicates with the administration.

👩‍💻

Office Worker

Processes new student registrations, updates database records, and manages the logistical school schedule.

Level 2: Layered Architecture

Part 2 — How Does It Work?

Visualizing the system as 4 distinct layers — each with a specialized purpose.

🖥️

The Interface

"The Front Desk"

High-performance Single Page App with 8 core modules: Dashboard, Students, Staff, Classes, and more.

🧠

The Brain

"The Sorting Office"

Central DataContext maintains real-time application state, ensuring UI updates are instantaneous across all views.

📡

The Messenger

"The Courier Hub"

Decoupled services layer that handles business logic and shuttles data between memory and disk.

⚡

Flash Engine

"The Fast Storage"

Optimized localStorage driver that persists your data on your local device for zero-latency offline performance.
